Our Story

Founded in 1995, JCL Production has been serving the Concepcion community with authentic, high-quality processed meats. What began as a small family business has grown into a trusted local brand, while maintaining our commitment to traditional recipes and handcrafted quality.

We source our meats from trusted local farmers and use only natural ingredients in our products. No artificial preservatives, just honest-to-goodness flavor.
